Mishai

Ingredents

  • Bread
  • Consentrated milk
  • mionese
  • Egg
  • Cabbage
  • Groundnut oil

Steps

  1. put Groundnut oil in the frying pan on fire for like 1 min
  2. cut the bread in half opening like a book and place on the hot oil in the pan on fire
  3. put in a fried egg in the bread abd add mionese and milk and cabbage
  4. close the bread and press the bread together in the hot oil till tje bread back is brown, and you are good to go, eat and enjoy
